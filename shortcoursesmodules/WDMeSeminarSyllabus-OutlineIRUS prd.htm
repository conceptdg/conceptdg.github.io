Motivation:

Evolution of DWDM Optical Networking, during the last decade, has revolutionized the telecommunications industry. The increased capacity, especially in the core network has kept up with the demand brought about by the growth of the Internet. Due to the Telecommunications Act of 1996, however, the capacity growth at the edge of the network (metro and access) has not kept up with the growth in the core à leading to bandwidth misdistribution. In the next few years, lower-cost optical networking is expected to continue the revolution at the edge in order to meet the demands due to introduction of new applications.
